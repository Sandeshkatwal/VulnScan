# Demo Walkthrough

Use this 5-minute Interview Walkthrough with Portfolio Demo Mode enabled.

Opening line:

> VulScan is not an exploit framework. It is an authorised assessment and reporting platform that focuses on evidence quality, OWASP mapping, manual validation, and safe reporting.

## 1. Dashboard Home, 30 seconds

Introduce VulScan as an OWASP-focused vulnerability assessment and reporting platform for authorised testing, local labs, and defensive workflows. Point out summary cards, platform modules, and the recommended workflow.

## 2. OWASP Report Matrix, 1 minute

Open the OWASP section and show the A01-A10 coverage matrix. Explain that demo indicators are simulated and require manual validation before stronger wording is used.

## 3. Authenticated Assessment Safety, 45 seconds

Show authenticated assessment sections: Auth Context, Authenticated Crawl, Role & Permission Mapping, A01 Manual Test Planner, Parameter Replay Planner, and Business Logic Review. Emphasise redacted context and no raw cookies, tokens, or passwords.

## 4. Evidence Vault, 1 minute

Open Evidence Vault and describe Redacted Demo Evidence, Evidence Quality Score, timeline events, and export safety checks. Explain that reports link evidence IDs instead of exporting unsafe raw evidence.

## 5. Finding Builder And Report Composer, 1 minute

Open Finding Builder and Report Composer. Show candidate wording, Business Impact, Technical Impact, Developer Remediation, Evidence References, Retest Status, Executive Summary, and Safe Export.

## 6. Closing, 45 seconds

Close with the safety model, local-first architecture, Python 3.11/FastAPI backend, React/TypeScript dashboard, and roadmap for richer templates and review workflows.
